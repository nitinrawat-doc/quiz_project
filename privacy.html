Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Quick Summary: We value your privacy. Quiz Sharp does not collect or store any personal information from users. All data is stored locally in your browser.

Information We Collect

Quiz Sharp is designed with privacy in mind. We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. The only data stored is:

  • Local Storage Data: Your quiz scores and leaderboard data are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Session Data: Temporary quiz session information stored in your browser's memory while you play.

User Privacy

No Login Required: Quiz Sharp does not require registration, login, or any personal information to use the platform. You can play anonymously or enter any name you prefer.

No Tracking: We do not use cookies for tracking purposes, and we do not monitor your browsing behavior or quiz activities beyond your current session.

Third-Party Services

Quiz Sharp uses the following third-party services:

  • Open Trivia Database (OpenTDB): We fetch quiz questions from OpenTDB API. Please refer to their privacy policy for information about their data practices.
  • CDN Services: We use Content Delivery Networks (Cloudflare CDN) to load external libraries like Particles.js and Font Awesome. These services may collect basic usage statistics.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser's localStorage API to save your:

  • Quiz scores and leaderboard rankings
  • Your chosen username (if provided)

This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted to our servers, your information remains secure on your device. However, please note that localStorage data can be accessed by other scripts running on the same domain, so avoid entering sensitive personal information.

Children's Privacy

Quiz Sharp is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, parents can feel confident allowing their children to use the platform.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of Quiz Sharp after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
